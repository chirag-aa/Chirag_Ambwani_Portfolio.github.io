  5. Pothole And Hump Detection on roads

    This circuit provides relevant information about the conditions of roads and can prevent damage to our vehicles. An ultrasonic sensor is used to identify potholes and humps and also measure their depth and height, as soon as the pothole is detected a message is displayed on the LCD and GSM module sends an SMS to the driver as well as the concerned authority.
